❓ FAQ

Got Questions?

When do my points expire? ▾
Points are valid for 12 months from the date they were earned. As long as you make at least one order per year, your points will never expire.
Can I use points in-store and online? ▾
Yes! You can redeem BK Rewards points at any participating outlet, on our website, or through the BK app. Just scan your QR code at checkout.
How quickly are points added to my account? ▾
Points are added to your account within minutes of your order being confirmed. For in-store purchases, they're applied as soon as the cashier scans your QR code.
What can I redeem points for? ▾
You can redeem points for menu items, upgrades, free delivery, and exclusive merchandise. The redemption catalogue updates every month with new options.
Is there a joining fee? ▾
BK Rewards is completely free to join. We'll even give you 100 welcome points the moment you sign up — enough to start redeeming immediately.
