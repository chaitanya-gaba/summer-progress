I Miss You

Har raat sochta hoon, aaj dil sambhal jaayega… par dil bhi na, suna nahi karta — aadat lag gayi hai tumhari. ❤️‍🔥

Tumhari wo hasi… jaise kisi ne roshni ko shape de di ho, aur wo aankhein — they don’t just look, they *command*. Ek baar jo inmein kho gaya… fir wapas nahi aata. 😉

Tum jab paas hoti ho, lagta hai waqt ruk sa gaya hai, aur jab door jaati ho, main sirf ek hi dua maangta hoon — “thoda ruk ja, dil abhi bhara nahi.” 💫

Kya karoon yaar, dil toh pagal hai — samjhta sab kuch hai, par maanta kuch bhi nahi. Bas itna samajh lo… agar pyaar gunaah hai, toh main repeat offender hoon. 🔥
